Getting Around Tbilisi

Comprehensive transport guide for expats. Navigate Tbilisi's metro, buses, taxis, and more with confidence and ease.

Metro System

Tbilisi's efficient metro system with 2 lines covering major areas

Key Information:

  • Operating hours: 6:00 AM - 12:00 AM
  • Cost: 1 GEL per ride with transport card
  • Lines: Akhmeteli-Varketili (Line 1) and Saburtalo (Line 2)
  • Key stations: Rustaveli, Freedom Square, Marjanishvili

City Buses

Extensive bus network covering all neighborhoods

Key Information:

  • Operating hours: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM (most routes)
  • Cost: 1 GEL with transport card, 1.20 GEL cash
  • Popular routes: #37 (Airport), #61 (Mtatsminda), #71 (Vake)
  • Real-time tracking available via mobile apps

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip:

Download the Tbilisi Transport app for real-time schedules

Taxis & Ride-sharing

Multiple taxi options from traditional to app-based services

Key Information:

  • Bolt: Most popular ride-sharing app
  • Yandex Taxi: Alternative ride-sharing option
  • Traditional taxis: Negotiate price beforehand
  • Average cost: 5-15 GEL for city center trips

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip:

Use Bolt or Yandex for transparent pricing and safety

Airport Transport

Multiple ways to get to/from Shota Rustaveli Tbilisi International Airport

Key Information:

  • Bus #37: Direct to city center (1 GEL)
  • Taxi: 25-40 GEL to city center
  • Bolt/Yandex: 20-35 GEL to city center
  • Airport is 20km from city center

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip:

Bus #37 is the most economical option with good frequency
